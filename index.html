Intelligence Assessment · 2022–2025
Crime Intelligence Analysis
🔴
Most Dangerous States
🏆
Uttar Pradesh leads with 6.88L IPC cases (2022) — 11.8% of India's total. Mission Shakti improved women's safety but violent crime remains high.
🥈
Maharashtra #2 — 4.66L cases. Mumbai, Pune, Nagpur account for 68% of state's crime. Cyber fraud is surging fastest.
⚔️
Madhya Pradesh has India's highest murder rate per lakh — 3.5 per 1L vs national avg of 2.2.
👩
Rajasthan leads crimes vs women — 40,738 cases. Rape and kidnapping rates disproportionately high.
✅
Safest States & Cities
🏔️
Nagaland, Mizoram, Sikkim consistently rank safest by crime rate per lakh. Community policing and small populations help.
🏙️
Pune ranks top 3 safest metros by crime-per-capita despite being India's 8th largest city. Planned development helps.
⚖️
Kerala's conviction rate — 62.8% is best among large states, the strongest deterrent India has.
🌿
Northeast India (excl. Assam, Manipur) has significantly lower IPC rates than national average.
💻
Emerging Threats
📈
Cyber crime up 500%+ since 2016. Jharkhand's Jamtara and Rajasthan's Mewat are now India's cybercrime capitals.
📱
UPI & OTP fraud account for 67% of all cyber complaints. Elderly and first-time smartphone users are prime targets.
🚗
Vehicle theft endemic — 1.7L cases in 2022. Delhi NCR & Mumbai are hotspots; recovery rates only 21%.
🤖
AI-powered crimes emerging: deepfake fraud, voice cloning scams projected to dominate stats by 2026.
📊
Data Gaps & Limitations
⚠️
Under-reporting is massive. NCRB estimates only 1 in 3 crimes are registered. Rural areas and UP/Bihar see the worst under-reporting.
🔄
Cyber crime charge-sheet rate is just 7.2% nationally — out of every 100 cyber FIRs, fewer than 8 result in prosecution.
🗓️
2022 is latest NCRB data. 2023–24 figures on this dashboard are projections based on state police quarterly data.
🗺️
Area-level data is estimated from published police zone reports and is indicative, not exact census-level data.
